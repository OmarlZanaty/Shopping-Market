Privacy Policy

App Name: Shopping Market
Package: com.almobarmg.shoppingmarket
Last updated: June 17, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Shopping Market ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Phone number: Used for account registration and login via OTP verification.
  • Name and address: Collected during checkout to deliver your orders.
  • Order history: We store your past orders to let you track and review them.
  • Device information: We may collect device type and OS version for app performance and crash reporting.
  • Camera / Barcode scanner: Used only when you choose to scan a product barcode. No images are stored.
  • Biometric data: The app may request fingerprint authentication to unlock the app. This is processed entirely on your device; we never receive or store biometric data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process and deliver your orders.
  • To send order status notifications (push notifications).
  • To improve app performance and fix issues.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data only with:

  • Delivery personnel assigned to your order (name and address only).
  • Cloud infrastructure providers (Google Cloud) for secure data storage and hosting.

4. Data Security

We use HTTPS encryption for all data transmitted between the app and our servers. Account access is protected by OTP verification and optional biometric authentication.

5. Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

6. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at the email below.
